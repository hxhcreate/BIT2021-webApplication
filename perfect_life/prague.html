Prague-style tour

Synopsis


Prague is the capital and largest city of the Czech Republic. It is located in Central Bohemia and the voltava River Basin. Located in the center of the European continent, the city has always played an important role in transportation and has close ties with neighboring countries.

Prague is a famous tourist city. It has a large number of buildings in various historical periods and styles, especially baroque style and Gothic style. Prague architecture gives an overall impression that the top of the building is particularly rich in changes and brilliant colors. It is known as one of the most beautiful cities in Europe and the first city in the world to be designated as a world cultural heritage

The city of 100 spires, Prague has enchanted visitors for centuries

The bohemian allure and fairytale features of Prague make it a perfect destination for culture vultures who want to immerse themselves in history, art, music, and architecture. You could devote an entire day to exploring Prazsky hrad (Prague Castle), then refuel over a hearty dinner at a classic Czech tavern. Spend some time wandering Old Town Square before heading over to the Astronomical Clock as it performs a mechinical promenade on the hour. Prague’s best bars are often historic pubs where you can spend the better part of a night tipping back central Europe's top-shelf beers.

Natural Scenery

In Prague under the setting sun, the scale of the buildings, red tiles and yellow walls are dazzling in the warm spring sun. In the distance, the voltava river is sparkling and the distant bridge is thin. The high and low roofs and spires on the castle mountain on the other bank are like a layer of gold powder, shining with dreamlike light. A world in fairy tales, an absurdly beautiful Prague.

Cultural Scenery

The city has Charles University (also known as Prague University), College of engineering, conservatory of music and other institutions of higher learning, as well as National Academy of Sciences, Academy of Agricultural Sciences and other scientific research institutions. There are many theatres, museums and art galleries. There are various styles of ancient buildings, including Prague Castle, palace, church, etc. The annual Prague Spring Concert is an important music event in the world. There are many parks and green spaces in the city.
